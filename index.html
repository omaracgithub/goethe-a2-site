Frequently asked questions

What is the Goethe-Zertifikat A2?

The Goethe-Zertifikat A2 certifies elementary German proficiency. It proves you can communicate in routine situations — shopping, work basics, and everyday social interactions. Many employers and visa offices accept it as proof of basic German ability.

How long is the Goethe A2 exam?

The written portion (reading, listening, writing) takes approximately 90 minutes. The speaking exam adds about 15 minutes, conducted in pairs or small groups. Total time is around 105 minutes.

What score do I need to pass?

You need 60 out of 100 points overall. Both the written module and the speaking module must be passed separately — you can't compensate a failed module with the other.

How does the AI writing review work?

Write your response to the prompt, then get instant feedback on grammar, vocabulary, coherence, and task fulfilment. The AI scores your work against A2 criteria and suggests specific improvements.
