Goethe A2 Exam Format: Complete Guide

June 10, 2026·Goethe A2

Key takeaways

  • Goethe A2 certifies CEFR A2 (elementary).
  • Four skills: Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking — 100 points total.
  • Typical duration: ~90 min written + ~15 min speaking.
  • This guide lives on a2.goetheprep.com — the A2 prep site.

Preparing for Goethe A2? This guide on a2.goetheprep.com explains the official A2 structure. The certificate proves elementary German for everyday situations, jobs that need basic communication, some visa pathways, and the step up to B1.

What the A2 exam tests

The Goethe-Zertifikat A2 covers four skills. The written part runs about 90 minutes; speaking is a separate paired session of around 15 minutes.

ModuleTimePartsWhat you do
Lesen~30 min4Read short emails, ads, notices, and simple articles
Hören~30 min4Follow short announcements, conversations, and radio clips
Schreiben~30 min2Write a short message and a personal note (everyday topics)
Sprechen~15 min3Introduce yourself, ask and answer questions, plan something together

Topics stay close to daily life: family, work, shopping, travel, health, and free time. You will use the past tense (Perfekt) and simple connectors, not complex argumentation.

Scoring

The exam is scored out of 100 points and you need 60 to pass. The four skills carry equal weight, so a weak module pulls your total down fast. See our passing score guide for the full breakdown.
